P1: Is the Sign intact?

YES. Home health agencies continue to operate. Aides go to patient homes. Medicare billing proceeds. The facility is licensed and inspected. All observable functions continue.

Critical interpretation: Sign integrity is evidence of successful Triadic Overwrite, not evidence against it. If you answer P1 with “yes, the institution still functions,” the analysis must proceed — because Sign preservation is the defining feature of a successful parasitic overwrite. The institution “working” is not exculpatory. It is the structural camouflage that makes the overwrite invisible from outside.

P2: Has the Signal been manipulated?

The budget allocation channel is the actual mechanism translating Medicare revenue into care decisions. This is the Signal: how resources flow from the billing function to staffing, wages, supplies, and maintenance. The PE fund, management company, and REIT have achieved P-adic proximity to this channel through acquisition — they are inside the financial cluster controlling every budget decision.

The P-adic access route: acquisition places the ownership structure inside the financial cluster. The LLC structure separates legal accountability from financial control — the entity bearing operational liability is not the entity controlling the budget. This separation is not an accident or a complexity. It is the structural instrument through which P-adic proximity is maintained while legal accountability is externalized.

P3: Has the Interpretant been replaced?

ORIGINAL INTERPRETANT
Medicare revenue = investment in care capacity enabling aide wages, staffing levels, and quality patient outcomes. The goal-function: maximize care quality within available resources.
REPLACED INTERPRETANT
Medicare revenue = extraction vehicle maximizing management fee, debt service, and REIT return, subject to regulatory compliance constraints. The goal-function: maximize extraction within compliance bounds.

The gap between these two Interpretants is μ = 0.82. The mechanism sustaining the replacement is σ = 0.62 (accumulated normalization of inadequate staffing). The magnitude is ε = 0.636. These are not moral judgments. They are measurements of the distance between what the institution nominally does and what the decision architecture actually optimizes.

1. Original Interpretant: Medicare revenue = investment in care capacity.

Formally derived responsible party: the actor achieving P-adic proximity and executing Interpretant replacement. Not the administrator. Not the aide. Not the patient. Regulatory design failure (ρ and μ): VBP produces +$1,500/year. The payment architecture is a T2 intervention whose Interpretant has been replaced from “structural accountability will follow” to “compliance theater will follow.”

The key improvement over Sign-level analysis: A Sign-level Situation says “employers pay low wages.” The P-SAGE Situation says “the budget allocation channel has been accessed by ownership achieving P-adic proximity through acquisition, and the Interpretant has been replaced from care investment to extraction vehicle.” These descriptions lead to completely different interventions. The first suggests wage policy (T2). The second identifies the access mechanism that must be addressed (T1).

Grounds: Four Traditions, One Structural Finding

Care Ethics: The web of care relationships is violated not by aide failure but by the structural architecture absorbing care capacity before it reaches the patient. The extraction architecture treats relational care knowledge as having zero economic value — the billing rate reflects the hour, not the relationship. High turnover destroys the relationship at exactly the moment continuity matters most. FHRI_max = 14 is an institutional commitment to providing the appearance of care while structurally preventing its substance.
Fairness / Justice: Rawls behind the veil of ignorance: no rational actor would design the home health extraction architecture if they faced equal probability of being the PE investor, the aide, Mrs. Chen, or her daughter. The veil test fails decisively. Even on Nozick’s libertarian grounds, the extraction architecture has a problem: Medicare reimbursement is a public contract for care delivery. The extraction architecture diverts public funds from care to financial return — a contractual violation of the terms under which public funds were allocated.
Consequentialism: Non-profit agencies (FHRI_max = 62.9): DTC 74.9%, turnover 22%, better care continuity. Proprietary (FHRI_max = 37.3): DTC 72.1%, turnover 35%, lower continuity. PE-acquired NH (FHRI_max = 13.6): 11% increased mortality, 33 fewer care minutes daily. The aggregate consequence of the extraction architecture across 12,239 agencies and 8M+ patients annually is quantified in hospitalizations, quality-adjusted life years, and preventable deaths. Consequentialism provides the strongest grounds for T1 structural reform because the outcome differences are now formally measured.
Virtue / Duty: Aristotle: the home health agency’s telos (proper function) is quality care enabling patient independence and dignity. PE acquisition corrupts the telos by substituting external goods (financial return) for the internal goods (excellence in care) that define the practice. Kant: could the extraction architecture be universalized? If every care institution were configured as PE-acquired facilities are, care as a meaningful category would cease to exist. Universalization fails. Maria’s duty is categorical — she fulfilled it 24 times in incident reports. The structural wrong is not her failure.
Capabilities (Nussbaum): Maria has formal freedom to choose her employer. She lacks substantive freedom to choose a structurally different work relationship — because the extraction architecture defines the terms of employment across most of the sector. This is formal freedom inside structural extraction: not freedom, but the appearance of freedom with the substance removed. The contractor classification in the gig economy makes this explicit: the Interpretant of “worker has freedom to choose their hours” has replaced “worker has employment security and capability floor.” The Triadic Hack applied to the concept of freedom itself.
