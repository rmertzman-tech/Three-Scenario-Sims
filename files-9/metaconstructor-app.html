Your PRF

The Phenomenological Reference Framework is the lens through which you perceive everything. You did not choose it consciously. But you can examine it.

04
Finish this sentence: "The way I was raised taught me that people who struggle financially do so because ______."
The automatic answer that comes first — not what you believe on reflection.
05
Name one belief about yourself that you have held for a long time and are no longer sure is true.
The beliefs we are most certain about are often the ones most worth examining.
06
Where does your emotional reaction to a situation most often not match what you actually think about it on reflection?
This gap between automatic response and considered judgment is the PRF made visible.
The PRF and the parasiteInstitutions can rewrite your PRF without your knowledge. If you have worked in a high-extraction environment, its assumptions about what is normal may have been absorbed into your PRF. The course calls this Dark Coherence: a functioning self whose goal-function has been replaced without awareness.

The Institutional Mirror

Apply P-SAGE to the institution from Section 1. The three Perception questions show what the institution is doing to your coordination capacity.

P-SAGE: The Three QuestionsP1 — Is the Sign intact? Does the institution appear to function normally?
P2 — Has the Signal been manipulated? What channel carries resources to decisions — and who controls it?
P3 — Has the Interpretant been replaced? What does the institution actually optimize for, versus what it says?
P1
Does your institution appear to function normally? Does it seem to serve its stated purpose from the outside?
Sign integrity is consistent with Triadic Overwrite, not evidence against it.
P2
What is the actual channel through which resources, recognition, and opportunities flow in your institution? Who controls that channel?
Follow the money, the attention, the approval. Where do they actually go?
P3
What does your institution actually optimize for in its daily decisions? Is that the same as its stated mission? State both Interpretants: original and replaced.
Original: what it says it does. Replaced: what it actually optimizes for.

The Meta-Constructor Session

Work with a reflective partner to examine the model that is running you — and begin revising it toward greater coherence with your Identity Kernel.

The Meta-Constructor Capacity is your ability to examine the framework through which you perceive your situation. You cannot escape your biology. But you are a system complex enough to include yourself as an object of your own attention — and to act differently as a result.

How this worksDescribe your situation to the reflective partner. It has read everything you have written throughout this practice. It will ask you questions that help you see your own model more clearly. Be honest — the quality of the reflection depends entirely on what you bring.
MC
Welcome. I have read what you have written throughout this practice.

Before we begin: what is the thing you most want to understand about your situation right now? Not what you think you should understand — the thing that actually keeps you up at night, or that you avoid thinking about because it is too uncomfortable.

Where You Are Aiming

Constructor Ethics does not end with diagnosis. It ends with direction.

Wangari Maathai did not fight the government. She planted trees. The Meta-Constructor principle is not about escaping your structural situation through heroism. It is about identifying, with precision, what you can build from exactly where you are.

The three levelsPerception: See clearly what is structurally happening.
Protection: Recognize when an institution is consuming your coherence before you are too depleted to act.
Purpose: Identify something worth building toward — a genuine commitment, a forward trajectory that is yours.
