UCP Metrics Reference

The UCP engine measures coordination health using three core metrics. Adjust scenario parameters below to see how organizational structures affect each one.

λ Raw Coordination Capacity Raw potential for agents to work together, based on trust and shared capability.
λ Perceived Experienced Coordination How much coordination agents actually experience — reduced by power gaps and fear.
Friction Coordination Loss Energy lost to hierarchy, distrust, and mis-aligned incentives. High friction = ethical risk.
